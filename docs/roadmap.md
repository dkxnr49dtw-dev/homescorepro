## Risk Management

### Technical Risks
- **Data accuracy**: Implement verification system, multiple sources
- **Scalability**: Plan infrastructure early, use cloud services
- **Security**: Regular audits, penetration testing

### Business Risks
- **Competition**: Focus on transparency USP, build community
- **Regulation**: Legal review, compliance monitoring
- **Market changes**: Stay flexible, monitor trends

### Mitigation Strategies
- Build strong relationships with data providers
- Diversify revenue streams
- Maintain healthy runway (12+ months)
- Regular user feedback loops
- Agile development process
