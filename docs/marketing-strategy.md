## üö® RISK MITIGATION

### Potential Risks & Solutions

1. **Risk:** Low conversion (free ‚Üí paid)
   **Solution:** Add more upgrade triggers (comparison limit, save limit, export paywall)

2. **Risk:** High churn rate
   **Solution:** Email drip campaign during trial, exit surveys, improve onboarding

3. **Risk:** Data accuracy concerns
   **Solution:** Publish data sources, update frequency, accuracy disclaimers

4. **Risk:** Competition from Domain/REA
   **Solution:** Focus on transparency & methodology (they can't/won't explain)

5. **Risk:** Limited to Melbourne only
   **Solution:** Expand to Sydney (Month 12), Brisbane (Month 18)
