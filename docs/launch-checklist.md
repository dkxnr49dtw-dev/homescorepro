## ğŸš¨ Launch Risks & Contingencies

### Technical Issues

**Risk**: Site crashes due to traffic
- **Mitigation**: Use CDN, test load capacity
- **Contingency**: Have upgrade plan ready

**Risk**: Payment processor fails
- **Mitigation**: Test thoroughly, have backup
- **Contingency**: Offer manual invoicing

**Risk**: Data inaccuracies reported
- **Mitigation**: Verify sources, add disclaimers
- **Contingency**: Quick update process

### Marketing Issues

**Risk**: Low conversion rate
- **Mitigation**: A/B test pricing/copy
- **Contingency**: Offer limited discount

**Risk**: Negative feedback
- **Mitigation**: Quick response, fix issues
- **Contingency**: Acknowledge and improve

**Risk**: Low traffic
- **Mitigation**: Diversify channels
- **Contingency**: Increase ad spend
