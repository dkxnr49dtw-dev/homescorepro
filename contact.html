Frequently Asked Questions

How does HomeScorePro calculate scores?
HomeScorePro uses 38+ data points for A-Scores (suburb analysis) and 23+ data points for B-Scores (property analysis). All calculations are transparent and based on verified data from ABS, Victoria Police, and other trusted public data sources. See our About page for more details.
Is my data secure?
Yes. We take privacy seriously. Property evaluations are stored locally in your browser (localStorage) with your permission. For paid users, data can be stored securely on our servers. See our Privacy Policy for details.
